Catalog summary

These catalogs contain observational measurements for galaxies in the five CANDELS fields, along with a compilation of derived physical properties such as redshift, stellar mass, etc. These standardized "theory friendly" catalogs are described in more detail in Somerville et al. (2020) and in the TK:supplementary material. Please see those documents for recommended selection criteria.
